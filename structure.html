Organization Profile

Purvanchal Lok Utthan Avam Shodh Parishad is a non-profit organisation, established in 1993 that works primarily in the domain of Education, Food & Nutrition, Business & Finance, Technology, Human Rights, Health, Energy & Environment, Child & Youth Development, Art & Culture and Agriculture. As of today, the organization has more than 2000 dedicated members and more than 20 thousand sympathizers.
